Twitter Aviary is a work in
progress web app, that visualizes
a Twitter account. 

The account searched will be 
based on their followers count, 
followers/following ratio and 
average Tweets per day.

If the account shows up as a bot
on the website botornot.net the
bird will also be drawn as a bot.

Aviary also uses Twitters Search,
Users LookUp, and Status APIs.
